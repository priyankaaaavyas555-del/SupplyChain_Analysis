# SupplyChain_Analysis
1)Title-->Sales Analysis of Supply Chain Data
2)Punch line-->The visualisation simply fortails to improve upon sales margins and profit margins of the ecommerce goods.
3)Description-->The Sales Analysis enables the decision makers to understand the overall parameters to enhance the profit of a particular product category "subcategory wise".It tells which regions are having higher profits and sales of a particular good.It also tells the ratio of how much product is purchased is to how much proportions are returned.This can help to change the overall scenario of the product landscape focussing on larger pictures of making the profit per quantity sold better.
4)The tech stack used are;_
Power BI desktop-->For report building process
Power Query-->Data Cleaning and Munging
DAX-->Data Analytical Expressions for conditional outcomes
Data Modelling--->For making fact tables connected with dimension tables
Extension:--> .pbix
DataSource:Real time data  of supply chain with changed statistics to maintain confidentiality
5)Features and Highlights:
Business problem-->Low profit margins
Goal of dashboard-->Provide data driven for increasing the sales and profit values
6)Key Visualisations
Cards-->KPI's like sales,profit and quanity
clustered columns charts-->segment wise sales and profit
100% Stacked Bar Chart-->SubCategory wise  data distribution
Stacked Area Chart--Comparing profits region wise
filters-->For filtering certain piece of data 
slicers-->User driven data priorities
elements-->texts and headers
Filled maps-->sales analysis over geographical panaroma
Line and Stacked Column Charts-->Delving deeper into sales increase
Funnel Charts-->Top product profits
Donut Charts-->Contribution in profits and sales accross diffrent zones,category
